We are building a conversational AI travel agent that lets users plan and book flights (and later hotels and experiences) through natural chat, just like talking to ChatGPT. After a one-time setup where users enter their loyalty programs, credit cards, and travel preferences, the app remembers everything, creates companion profiles, suggests optimized flight options, and books them instantly in the same conversation using secure stored details. It delivers real-time iMessage-style alerts for delays or changes and learns from post-trip feedback to improve future recommendations, delivering a seamless, personalized experience that combines smart planning with frictionless execution.

The development of an intelligent, conversational travel booking platform begins with a rigorous requirements engineering phase that translates the described user journey into precise functional and non-functional specifications. Core capabilities include natural-language intent capture for destination and travel parameters, one-time ingestion of airline loyalty identifiers and tokenized payment instruments, persistent user preference modeling, dynamic companion profile creation, multi-option flight recommendation generation, atomic instant booking execution, post-trip experiential learning loops, and seamless expansion pathways to ancillary services. Non-functional priorities encompass sub-second conversational latency, end-to-end PCI-DSS and GDPR compliance, horizontal scalability to millions of concurrent sessions, fault-tolerant transaction processing, and measurable superiority in fare optimization over commodity aggregators through hybrid data sources and predictive modeling.
